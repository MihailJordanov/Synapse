## 🔹 About the Game

**Synapse** is a logic-driven strategy card game that fuses **graph theory** with dynamic card mechanics and tactical decision-making.  
Each card has two key traits:  
- **Type** – its core identity (e.g., Fire, Nature, Mechanic, etc.)  
- **Interactions** – elements it can connect or react with  

When cards with **matching interactions** are placed together on the board, they form **graph connections**.  
Your goal is to **build cycles** within this graph — each closed cycle earns points based on the level’s rules and difficulty.

You’ll progress through levels, face off against clever **AI opponents and unique bosses**, unlock new cards, and master increasingly complex logical patterns.

---

## 🧩 Gameplay Concept

- 🧠 **Logic Meets Strategy** – every move matters; one wrong card can break your entire network.  
- 🔗 **Graph Connections** – cards form nodes and edges in an evolving, dynamic graph.  
- ♻️ **Cycle Building** – complete loops to score points and trigger combos.  
- 🎴 **Collect & Evolve** – unlock new cards, each with unique powers and interactions.  
- ⚔️ **Boss Fights & Challenges** – conquer levels that push both your brain and your creativity.
